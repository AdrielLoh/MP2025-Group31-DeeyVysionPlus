About Us

Welcome to DeepVision! We are a dedicated team of students from Temasek Polytechnic, passionately engaged in an innovative deepfake technology project. Our mission is to delve into the fascinating world of deepfake technology, exploring its vast potential and the significant implications it holds for society. Through our project, we aim to uncover the complexities of this technology, raise awareness about its ethical considerations, and contribute to the ongoing discourse on its impact on various aspects of life.
