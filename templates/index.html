The Purpose of DeepVysion+

As digital media becomes increasingly easy and cheap to manipulate, deepfakes pose an ever-increasing threat to individuals and organizations. DeepVysion+ provides an interactive platform for spreading awareness and exploring detection solutions.

Spread Awareness

We provide reading resources and mini-games for users to learn more about deepfakes, such as the process behind creating deepfakes, how to identify deepfakes, and how to protect themselves from deepfake threats.

Exploring Detection Solutions

Users can explore our deepfake detection models and solutions to learn more about how deepfakes can be detected by analyzing different video and audio features.

Protect

We hope that this project will educate people to protect themselves from deepfake frauds and threats, and that our detection solutions will drive future innovation in the deepfake detection field.
