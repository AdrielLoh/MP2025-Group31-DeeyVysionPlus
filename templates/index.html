The Purpose of DeepVysion+

As digital media is becoming increasingly easy and cheap to manipulate, deepfakes pose an ever increasing threat to individuals and organizations. Therefore, the purpose of DeepVysion+ is to provide an interactive platform for spreading awareness regarding deepfakes, and exploring different solutions in detecting deepfake media.

Spread Awareness

We provide reading resources and mini-games for users to learn more about deepfakes, such as the process behind creating deepfakes, how to identify deepafakes, and how to protect themselves from deepfakes.

Exploring Detection Solutions

Users can explore our deepfake detection models and solutions to learn more about how deepfakes can be detected by analysing different video / audio features.

Protect

We hope that this project will be able to educate people to protect themselves from deepfake frauds and threats, and that the deepfake detection solutions in this project will be able to drive future innovation efforts in the deepfake detection field.
