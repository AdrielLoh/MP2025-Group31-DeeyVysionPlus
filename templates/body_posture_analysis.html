How the Technology Works

Pose Estimation

We use MediaPipe Pose to extract full-body keypoints across time. These landmarks represent skeletal posture and joint visibility from the video frames.

Keypoint Analysis

We normalize and analyze spatial patterns of joint coordinates over time, computing statistical features like mean, std, and confidence range.

AI Classification

Our neural network analyzes posture-based features to classify videos as real or deepfake with high confidence, based on physical plausibility of body movements.
